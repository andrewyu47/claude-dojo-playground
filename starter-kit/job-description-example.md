# Lifecycle Marketing Manager — Hatch (fictional)

We're a 40-person SaaS company looking for someone to own lifecycle and email end to end.

## What you'll do
- Own the email and lifecycle program: onboarding, retention, win-back.
- Build and report on funnels; turn the numbers into decisions leadership trusts.
- Partner with content to ship campaigns weekly, not quarterly.
- Set up light automation so the program runs without heroics.

## What we're looking for
- 4+ years in email/lifecycle marketing.
- You've owned a channel and can show what moved because of you.
- Comfortable in analytics: you build the report, not just read it.
- Bonus: light HTML, and any experience using AI tools to move faster.

## Why it's a good role
Small team, real ownership, you'll see your work reach customers the same week you build it.
